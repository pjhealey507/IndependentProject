Title: *title*

*title* is a 2D top-down shooter with a time rewinding mechanic.
The player will travel through a procedurally generated level
while fighting enemies. The art style will be neon with heavy
use of particles with extra effects to emphasize the time 
reversal mechanic. The game will have permadeath and random
generation, making it fit into the roguelike genre.

Programming:
- Command pattern for time rewind
- Object pooling for projectiles
- Singletons for various management scripts
- Procedural generation

AI:
- Behavior trees
- Dynamic difficulty

Art:
- Bright, flashy, neon
- Particles for bullets, explosions, rewinds
- Ghost trail shaders
